Everything you need

A Complete Professional Ecosystem

From discovering alumni mentors to landing your dream job — AlumniConnect has all the tools to accelerate your career journey.

👥
Alumni Directory
Browse and connect with thousands of alumni across companies, industries, and experience levels. Filter by skills, location, or expertise.
💼
Job Board
Alumni post exclusive job openings, internships, and referrals directly for students from their college. Apply with one click.
💬
Direct Messaging
Send and receive messages directly with alumni or students. Build meaningful professional relationships that last beyond college.
📅
Events & Meetups
Attend networking nights, webinars, hackathons, and alumni reunions. Alumni can create and host events for the community.
🎓
Student Profiles
Showcase your CGPA, skills, projects, and more. Let alumni discover you based on your talents and academic performance.
🛡️
Admin Control
Powerful admin tools to manage users, moderate content, view analytics, export data, and keep the community safe and active.
Built for everyone

Your Role, Your Experience

AlumniConnect is designed specifically for three types of users — each with a tailored experience and powerful tools.

🎓
Students
Discover alumni from your college, explore job opportunities, attend career events, and get mentored by professionals who've walked your path.
  • Browse entire alumni directory
  • Apply to exclusive job postings
  • Message alumni directly
  • Attend networking events
  • Showcase skills & CGPA
Register as Student →
👔
Alumni
Give back to your alma mater. Post jobs from your company, mentor talented students, create events, and grow your professional legacy.
  • Post job openings & internships
  • Browse student talent pool
  • Create & host events
  • Message students directly
  • Build professional network
Register as Alumni →
🛡️
Admins
Manage the entire platform — users, content, analytics, and system settings. Keep the community thriving with powerful moderation tools.
  • Full user management panel
  • Suspend or activate accounts
  • View analytics & export CSV
  • Moderate jobs & events
  • Add new admin accounts
